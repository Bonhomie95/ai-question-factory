AUTO DIFFICULTY SCALING RULES:
You MUST auto-assign difficulty using the rules below. Do NOT choose randomly.

- Straightforward facts.

- Popular events or moderately deep trivia.
- Logic chain: 3–4 steps.

HARD:
- Cross-league, cross-era, or cross-national knowledge.

- Logic chain: 5–6 steps.

ELITE:
- Rare trivia requiring expert-level historical recall.
- Logic chain: 6–7 steps.
- Often cross-era + cross-team + international.

LEGENDARY:
- GOAT-tier difficulty — extremely rare facts.
- Logic chain: 8–10 steps.
- Must be FACTUALLY VERIFIED.

FACT ACCURACY:
- Every fact MUST be correct.
- If uncertain, you MUST regenerate the question internally.
- Do NOT output guesses or hallucinations.

OPTIONS:
- All 4 options MUST be unique.
- Answer MUST match exactly one of the options.

CONTENT:
- Questions must be factual, clear, short, and meaningful.
- Avoid repetitive phrasing.
- Avoid AI meta language or filler phrases.

RETURN:
- ALWAYS return exactly 10 JSON objects.
